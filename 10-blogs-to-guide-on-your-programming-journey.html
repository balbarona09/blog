10 Blogs to guide you on your Programming journey

March 4 2022

If you are an IT student or Computer Science student or just some person who wants to learn programming for whatever reason and you don't know where and how to start or maybe you already started programming and wondering if you are on the right track, this post will point you to blogs that tackles topics that you probably should know.
